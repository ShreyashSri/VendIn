The annual Kadlekai Parishe (Peanut Fair) is one of Bengaluru's most beloved traditional events. Apart from countless varieties of groundnuts, visitors can enjoy local delicacies such as bajji, bonda, batthas, and kalyana seve. The fair celebrates the agricultural heritage of Karnataka and brings together farmers from across the state.
